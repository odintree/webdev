Digital Production

TMPL

The thought of a well-functioning residential area has changed dramatically the last decade. We are in many ways shielded from our neighbors compared to past times, the connected living is fragmented. After some user-research, we understood that there is still a need to communicate and interact between neighbors.

By combining the social aspects with technology and environmental living, we conceptualized and produced a smart ecosystem for housing residents. A mobile platform for interaction between people within the same neighborhood, apartment complex or housing association.

The project was kick-started with concept development, and later prototyped into an interactive platform. As everything was in place and tested by potential users, the project jumped into production phase and followed by technical quality assurance. The production included everything from system architecture to UX/UI design.

We tested the first beta version(MVP), both in-house and with independent users. After showing great results, TMPL was trusted by one of the leading Real Estate contractors of sustainable and innovative buildings in Sweden.
